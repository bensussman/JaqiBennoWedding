Directions

*We would really love to minimize the number of cars being used during the weekend. If you need help finding a ride from the airport, are interested in splitting a rental car, or are renting a car and have some extra room to help drive other friends to Sebastopol or to and from any of our weekend events, please fill out our rideshare form here.*

For folks coming from the east coast, we recommend flying into San Francisco (SFO) or Oakland (OAK). From there, itâ€™s about an hour north drive to Sebastopol.

Folks coming in from the west coast, we recommend flying into Sonoma County Airport in Santa Rosa. There are direct flights from many major west coast cities, and the drive to Sebastopol is only 15 minutes or so.

From the airport, you can rent a car, split a rental with others arriving around the same time (check out our rideshare form), or take the Sonoma County Airport Express shuttle to the Santa Rosa Park & Ride (for more information and to buy tickets, click here). If you need help arranging a pickup from the shuttle, please let us know.
