Things to do in the area

There are so many great things to do in the area, here are some of our favorite things to do when visiting Sonoma County:

  • See beautiful redwoods and go hiking in Armstrong Woods.
  • Eat a cinnamon roll the size of your head at Wild Flour Bakery.
  • Go kayaking along the Russian River.
  • Head to the beach at Bodega Bay.
  • Eat at the taco trucks on Santa Rosa Avenue.
  • Visit the In-N-Out Burger in Rohnert Park.
  • Visit the In-N-Out Burger in Petaluma.
  • Visit the In-N-Out Burger next to SFO.
